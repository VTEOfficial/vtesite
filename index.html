Active Defense

Interception happens
at the event layer.

Vertex does not poll for changes. It listens to gateway events and evaluates every destructive action the moment the intent is registered — before execution reaches your server state.

  • Mass ban and kick interception
  • Channel and role deletion prevention
  • Webhook creation and abuse blocking
  • Unauthorized bot additions
  • Administrator privilege escalation
  • Automatic lockdown on breach threshold
  • Trusted operator whitelist with zero-trust fallback
